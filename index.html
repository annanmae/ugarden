UGArden Herbs

Project Details

Purpose

To empower UGArden and enhance their operations, allowing them to better cultivate a community around sustainable gardening practices.

Mission

To develop an app that helps UGArden better organize and record the data they collect from their herb harvesting process.

Strategy

To create a user-friendly tool that simplifies and expedites the data collection and recordkeeping processes for all UGArden team members.
